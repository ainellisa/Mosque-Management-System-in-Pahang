Quranic Class

Our Kelas Al-Quran (Quran Classes) are designed to offer a comprehensive and enriching educational experience for individuals of all ages who seek to deepen their understanding and connection with the Quran.

  • ASAS AL-QURAN
  • KELAS TAJWID
  • ASAS TARANNUM
  • HALAQAH TALAQQID BERSANAD

We welcome individuals of all skill levels, whether you are a beginner or seeking to deepen your existing knowledge.

We believe that learning the Quran is a lifelong journey. Join us in this enriching experience as we strive to strengthen our connection with the Divine through the study of the Holy Quran.
