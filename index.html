About Us

eMosque Pahang System

eMosque Pahang is a comprehensive information system designed to consolidate and manage data on mosques across the Pahang state in Malaysia. It serves as a centralized hub for gathering details on events, activities, and the various services offered by these mosques, enhancing accessibility and connectivity for the local community.

More Details

Social & Community

Mosques play a central role in fostering social and community cohesion within the Muslim community

Education

mosques have educational programs aimed at providing religious and secular education

Facility

Mosques typically provide facilities for daily prayers, Friday congregational prayers (Jumu'ah), and special occasions.

Dakwah

Mosques often engage in dakwah activities by organizing outreach programs, seminars, and events that promote understanding and dialogue about Islam.

Services

Mosques provide a range of services to cater to the educational, and social needs of the Muslim community.

Hajj & Umrah Course

A Hajj and Umrah course in a mosque is an educational program specifically designed to provide guidance and information to Muslims who are planning to perform the Hajj and Umrah pilgrimages.

Charity & Donation

Mosques actively engage in charity and donation services as part of commitment to helping those in need and promoting social welfare.

Counseling and Support Services

Mosques provide counseling services to support individuals and families in times of personal or emotional challenges, offering guidance based on Islamic principles.

Mortuary Management Course

Educational program that focuses on the proper procedures and Islamic guidelines for handling and managing deceased individuals, from the moment of death to burial.

Qadi

The Qadi needs to ensure that the principles of marriage and dowry have been prepared so that the marriage goes smoothly.

Quranic Class

educational programs or courses focused on teaching the Quran, the holy book of Islam, to individuals of various ages.
