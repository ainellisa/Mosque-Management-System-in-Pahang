Counseling & Support Service

Our Counseling & Support Services aim to offer guidance, emotional assistance, and a helping hand to individuals and families facing various challenges in their lives. Rooted in Islamic principles, our dedicated team of counselors and support staff is committed to fostering a caring and compassionate environment for all.

  • Youth Counseling
  • Grief and Bereavement Support
  • Spiritual Counseling
  • Marriage and Family Counseling

If you or someone you know is in need of counseling or support, please contact us All discussions and interactions are treated with the utmost confidentiality and respect for your privacy.

our commitment to the well-being of our community goes beyond prayer and worship. We believe in creating a supportive and caring environment that reflects the teachings of Islam. Feel free to reach out, and let us walk this journey together.
